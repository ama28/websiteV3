overview

GridControl is a service that we designed for our client, WindStax Energy, a developer of renewable energy solutions. GridControl is a customer-facing mobile app for WindStax customers that makes managing and directing energy from Windstax turbines more accessible while simultaneously promoting sustainable behavior.

My greatest struggle with this project was gaining a strong understanding of users using limited data in a problem space that was completely unfamiliar to me; however, overcoming this obstacle pushed me to develop methods of prioritizing and scoping services without the need for constant user feedback.
