t(rack)


t(rack) is a startup proposal that strives to promote sustainability in the clothing industry. The t(rack) app is a used and new clothing marketplace that allows users to "track" the history of an outfit's production and its usage. I designed a logo for the company and created a prototype for its app.




PeerCoCo


PeerCoCo is a startup proposal that intends to match prospective international college students with current college students for counseling, in order to make the transition as smooth and comfortable as possible. I created a design document and logo, adhering to a modern feel for the younger age group while keeping in mind the company's emphasis on close collaboration.
